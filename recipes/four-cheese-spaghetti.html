Four Cheese Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti: 240 gr.
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Butter: 2 tbsp
  • Ricotta: 2 large spoonfuls
  • Feta Cheese: 2 ounces (60 gr.)
  • Asiago Cheese: 2 ounces (60 gr.) - grated
  • Parmesan Cheese: 2 ounces (60 gr.) - grated
  • Pepper

Directions

Cook spaghetti in salty water (1 tbsp of kosher salt)

In the meantime prepare the sauce:

In a saucepan heat oil and butter. Add Ricotta and Feta cheeses and cook on low until cheeses are melted.

When the pasta is cooked, add to the saucepan and stir well to incorporate the cheese.

Remove from heat. Add Asiago and Parmesan and stir well. Add some pepper. Serve.
